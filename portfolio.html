About Me

Lauren brings a unique background to the world of coding. After receiving an undergraduate degree in Elementary Education, Lauren taught Kindergarten for many years. After discovering yoga, Lauren earned her 200 and 500 hour Yoga Therapy certifications. Combing education and yoga, in 2014 Lauren co-founded MoveEd. MoveEd was hired by Minnesota school districts to teach teachers way to incorporate mindfulness in their classrooms.

After living in India and Medellin, Colombia, Lauren returned to Minnesota. Currently attending a full-stack coding course through the University of Minnesota. Previous Kindergarten teacher, current yoga teacher, to future coder!
